Message from the Dean

The Faculty of Information Science and Technology (FIST), located at East Africa University’s main campus, was established on September 15, 2007. In its founding year, the faculty admitted 50 students into the Bachelor of Computer Science program, with 45 successfully graduating in June 2010.

Since then, the faculty has grown remarkably. Today, we offer six undergraduate degree programs and proudly serve a student population of 140 as of September 2025, making us the largest faculty at East Africa University.

Our faculty is supported by a dedicated team of highly qualified local and international staff, trained both locally and abroad. They are committed to guiding students and ensuring programs are delivered efficiently.

We also maintain strong partnerships with governmental and non-governmental organizations, bridging the gap between academia and industry. Every program integrates industrial internships, providing students with valuable hands-on experience.

Together, we continue to build a brighter future through technology and innovation.
